Civil Cases

Civil cases involve disputes between individuals, organizations, or entities. They can include contract disputes, personal injury claims, property disputes, and more.

Criminal Cases

Criminal cases involve alleged violations of criminal law. They can range from minor infractions and misdemeanors to serious felonies like theft, assault, drug offenses, etc.

Family Cases

Family law cases deal with matters related to family relationships , such as divorce , child custody , child support , spousal support and alimony , and adoption of child.

Probate and Estate Cases

These cases involve the distribution of assets and the settlement of estates after a person's death. They can include wills of that person , trusts, and probate proceedings.

Juvenile Cases

Juvenile cases involve minors who are subject to the jurisdiction of the juvenile court system. These cases may include delinquency matters and dependency matters.

Administrative Cases

Administrative cases may involve disputes related to government agencies or regulatory bodies. For example, cases related to licensing, permits, or regulatory compliance.
